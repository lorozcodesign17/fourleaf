Design a responsive website for a concept café called Four Leaf Café & Bakery.

The café theme is luck and whimsical freshness with a Frutiger Aero aesthetic. The visual style should feel glossy, airy, playful, and optimistic. Include elements like bubbles, clouds, clovers, soft gradients, and nature imagery such as grass or sky. Small playful elements like goldfish icons, stars, and clovers can appear throughout the interface.

The overall feeling should be clean, fresh, modern, and slightly nostalgic, inspired by early 2000s Frutiger Aero visuals.
